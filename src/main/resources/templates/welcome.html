About Impression

Impressions is for every day users to document their opinion & impression of other users. Have you ever felt a deep urge to comment about someone but couldn't find a way to tell them? Here is a way to remain anonymous in your ratings & leave comments for everyday users & share your impression.

Grayscale includes full HTML, CSS, and custom JavaScript files along with SASS and LESS files for easy customization!
